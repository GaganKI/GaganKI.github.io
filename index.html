001 — About

Honest about
where I am

I'm a 6th semester B.E. AIML student who genuinely enjoys the process of building — not just the end result. What excites me is the intersection of ML systems and backend engineering: training a model is one thing, making it work reliably inside a real system is the actual challenge.

My strongest project is a Smart Pothole Detection system — a real pipeline combining computer vision, depth estimation, and a geospatial backend for city-level road analytics. It's not perfect, but it runs, and I understand every part of it.

I pick up new technologies fast — especially when the problem is genuinely interesting. I'm drawn to deep learning, system design thinking, and backend challenges with real constraints. I use AI tools to help me move faster, but I make sure I understand what I'm building.

I'm still figuring out my exact niche — but I know I want to work somewhere that takes ML seriously: fintech, AI-first startups, product companies where the systems actually matter.
